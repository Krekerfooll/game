Unity3D 2018.3.2f1 game for PC "Centipede"

Controls:
Moving: left-right arrows or "A" and "D" keys.
Firing: Left Mouse Button or an other key specified in ShootingScript on player's GameObject.

Description:
The visual part of the game represents a field on the bottom of which a player moves from left to right or vice versa. The player has to shoot enemies which may be static such as mushrooms or dynamic like a centipede. Enemies can spawn on the whole field except the player's part. Apart from thee playing field, the screen contains GUI which contains information about player's HP and other useful information.
